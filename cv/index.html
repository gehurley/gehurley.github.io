Current Role

Digital Preservation Librarian, Scholars Portal, Ontario Council of University Libraries/University of Toronto.

  • Lead the Scholars Portal digital preservation program to fulfill the Ontario Council of University Libraries’ mandate to preserve shared academic library collections and support member capacity.
  • Manage and grow the Permafrost hosted digital preservation service, including providing all front-line client onboarding, billing, training and support; writing documentation and guides; developing new features; and working with Scholars Portal’s systems staff to maintain the technical components.
  • Maintain the Scholars Portal Trustworthy Digital Repository documentation and certification and develop and maintain workflows for preserving shared collections, including journal articles, ebooks and new content types.
  • Develop and maintain policies, plans and workflows for the preservation of research data in Dataverse.
  • Review preservation terms in e-resources licenses and consult on licensing-related issues.
  • Consult with OCUL members to provide recommendations on digital preservation practices, develop new services and resources, and host training opportunities.
  • Participate in committees, working groups and research initiatives to collaborate and share knowledge about digital preservation regionally, nationally, and internationally.
  • Teach workshops and provide lectures and other outreach on digital preservation topics for various user communities.
  • Supervise students and staff in project support work.
  • Collaborate with systems staff, OCUL staff, and librarians across the unit to meet goals and develop services.
